The Science behind Lofi


Lofi has been known to be used as a studying type of music but why? Well, based on science and
experimentation on many different fronts, Lofi has been seen to reduce stress and improve
productivity. The elements that contribute to this are the jazzy instruments, the distinct
sound of the constant drum beat and the environmental sounds. It also acts as an effective
stimulant. It has a balance of engaging and non-distracting sounds that give you the music you
love yet, it is so simple that it does not side-track your mind and keeps you focused in a
closed-in environment.

How to Use This


So, how can you use this to your advantage? Depending what you are doing you can listen to lo fi
music to be more productive in whatever you are doing. For example, let’s say you are a student
needing to study. However, you have a problem with staying focused and think there is no possible
way you can do all that studying. Well, grab some headphones or speakers, put on a lofi soundtrack
and work your way through. You’ll find that this time it was easier to work. By using this music,
you in a way block out all distractions in your environment, keeping yourself more focused
(make sure to get rid of your phone out of sight). However, not all tasks are suitable for Lofi.
So make sure that you use it properly.
