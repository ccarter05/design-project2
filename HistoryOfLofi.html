History


The history of Lofi is very interesting. Lofi was first created when Youtube debuted live stream
channels on their platform in 2013. Lofi artists began creating their own Lofi playlists and
streaming services on youtube. One of the most notable pioneers and artists of Lofi was Nujabes
and J. Dilla. It all started with Nujabes’ soundtrack for the anime series Samurai Champloo in
2004. That is the reason why many Lofi playlist are connected to anime and animated film series.
Another popular artist is ChilledCow (which is now called Lofi Girl). In his debut in 2015 he was
able to lead Lofi forward almost acting as a Lofi hip-hop radio station with over 7.5 million
subscribers and 13,000 hours of listening after only two years have passed! That’s how Lofi
became a popular genre of music through Youtube.
